Fūrinkazan

After being introduced to the book Sun Tzu's Art of War, I became fascinated by his ideas, in particular Chapter 7, Fūrinkazan - As swift as the wind, as gentle as the forest, as fierce as fire, as unshakable as a mountain.

The colouring and aesthetic of this collection was inspired and based on these passages.

「 其 疾 如 風，其 徐 如 林，侵 掠 如 火，不 動 如 山，難 知 如 陰，動 如 雷 震 」

孫子兵法

God Jiang

Temple environment induces my spiritual awareness to convey the beauty of the Asian Culture. This collection using Asian images for main elements design, and silhouette is transformed by Asian pattern. Combine different fabric and colors on cutting yoke to convey Asian Art.

Trough transforming diverse mask patterns of the Asian Gods roles to the clothing, creating a ingenious combination of fashion and traditional art to present Gods.

「 忠 義 昭 天，懸 掛 在 宮 廟 的 匾 額 銘 記 在 我 們 心 中，為 此 想 對 陣 頭 文 化 有 所 貢 獻 」

董秉羲
